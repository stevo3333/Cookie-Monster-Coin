3. Select a Website Builder or CMS

  • No Coding Required:

  • Crypto Wallets: Add buttons to connect MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.
  • Blockchain Explorer Links: Let users verify token transactions.
  • Buy Button: Integrate with Pump.fun or DEX like Uniswap or PancakeSwap.

6. Deploy and Optimize

  • Test the website across devices (mobile, tablet, desktop).

7. Promote Your Website
